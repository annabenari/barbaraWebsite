Supervision

Having worked as a supervisor since 2005 and currently holding the position of Supervision Lead at a professional organisation, I find immense fulfilment in supporting fellow therapists. I work with an integrative therapeutic style, drawing from the various modalities to provide comprehensive support; encompassing academic knowledge, practical experience, and my own unique perspective. I am well versed in supporting professionals dealing with stress, overwhelm, and burnout, as well as clinical expertise with client-based issues.

While I am proficient in conducting online sessions, I prioritise in-person collaboration for its effectiveness in preserving the nuances of our professional relationship. However, I am flexible and open to accommodating online sessions when necessary.

For those who are considering group supervision, I have found it to be an effective means of support for therapists, and so I also encourage this style of supervision. I believe the collaborative approach promotes additional learning and is cost effective too! I work with small groups up to four and am happy to form a group with my referrals, or you are welcome to bring an existing group. I look forward to the potential opportunity to collaborate with you, whether in therapeutic support or professional supervision. Please feel free to contact me for further inquiries or to initiate a conversation about how we can work together.
