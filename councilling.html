Supervision

I have worked as a counselling supervisor since 2005 in various settings, including:

  • Supervising groups of volunteers on helplines
  • Guiding staff teams working with homeless youth
  • Supporting individual therapists in private practice and organizations
  • Training and supervising counselling and social work students
  • Providing supervision for teachers and school staff
  • Offering guidance to youth workers, foster carers, and pre-school staff

I adopt an integrative style, incorporating academic knowledge and practical experience. I specialize in supporting professionals dealing with stress, overwhelm, and burnout.

Whether you're a counsellor seeking supervision or another professional needing guidance, feel free to reach out for a chat to see if I'm the right fit for you.

I also have extensive experience leading small groups of therapists in supervision sessions. This collaborative approach not only enriches learning but also helps manage costs effectively.
