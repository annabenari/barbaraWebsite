Couples/Relationship Counselling

Relationships are the crux of life, however, can be rife with challenges. So often, many of these can be managed with the help of a therapeutic neutral 3rd party. The outcome may or may not be the happy ending we all hope for but the work can support with whatever the best outcome is for you, making the process easier to navigate. Importantly, the work with a therapist can offer the skills you want develop to enable a more positive future within the troubled relationship, whether that be with a partner, an extended family member, a child, or a friend.

If you think this might be something you want to know more about, or to consider as a means of improving your current relationship, understanding the dynamics at play, or working through some of the more challenging aspects of the relationship, please contact me to have an informal chat to see if you think we might be a good fit. I can answer any questions not covered here and arrange an initial session to further outline your needs and agree a way forward.
