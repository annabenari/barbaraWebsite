About Me

Welcome to my online space. I am genuinely delighted that you have found your way here, and I’m hopeful that as you explore, you’ll discover insights and options to guide your next steps. I hope too that this will give you a glimpse into my professional background, values, and the contributions I can make to your life’s journey.

With over 40 years of experience as a Qualified Social Worker, Psychotherapist, and Supervisor, my career has spanned diverse settings and roles. While my formal training and education took root in the United States, I’ve been practicing in the UK since 2002. As a Psychotherapist specializing in trauma, and my education being grounded in Systemic Social Work, I am uniquely positioned to benefit from the extensive and varied experiences which have enriched my knowledge, understanding, and expertise. This depth of experience has enhanced the supervision work and support I am able to offer to other Therapists.

Education and Training:

  • Bachelor of Arts – Psychology and Social Work, University of New Hampshire (1975)
  • Master of Social Work, Adelphi University (NY) (1982)
  • Licensed Clinical Social Worker - State of Georgia (1999)
  • Diploma in Counselling Supervision, Farnborough College (CACPB 2008)

Over the years, I’ve undertaken dozens of courses and workshops, covering a wide range of topics. Feel free to ask me if you wish to learn more about these.
