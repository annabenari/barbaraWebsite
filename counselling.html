Couples/Relationship Counselling

My interest and understanding of relationships, communication, and interpersonal dynamics has led me towards the work I do with couples and others in troubled relationships. I am passionate about my work and believe strongly that change and healing are not only possible, but necessary, if we feel that parts of our lives are simply less well than we’d like them to be.

I hold a profound belief that relationships lie at the core of our existence, yet they so often present challenges. If you find yourself caught in repetitive patterns, facing tension and distress, I can offer therapeutic support as a neutral third party. There are so many reasons that relationships struggle, and these are always worth exploring. While outcomes may not always align with expectations, the collaborative work we embark upon together, can facilitate change and acceptance. Working with a therapist will support the development of valuable skills to move you towards a more positive future within your relationships; be that with a partner, an extended family member, a child (including an adult child), or a friend.

If you resonate with this thinking and are considering exploration to enhance your current relationship dynamics, please reach out. We can initiate an informal discussion to assess our compatibility, followed by an initial session to outline your needs and chart a course forward.
