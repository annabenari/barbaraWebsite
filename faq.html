FAQ's

What is therapy and what does it do?

Psychotherapy (also called talk therapy) refers to a variety of treatments that aim to help a person identify and change troubling emotions, thoughts, and behaviors.

How much do you charge for counseling?

Couples/Relationship - £80 per session. Concessions can be considered for long-term agreements or financial hardship. Please ask about this if appropriate.

How much do you charge for Supervision?

Groups of up to 4 people will have a duration of 2 hours and cost £120.
