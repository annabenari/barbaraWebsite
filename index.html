About Me

Welcome to my online space.
I am genuinely delighted that you have found your way here, and I’m hopeful that as you explore, you’ll discover insights and options to guide your next steps. I hope too that this will give you a glimpse into my professional background, values, and the contributions I can make to your life’s journey. With over 40 years of experience as a Qualified Social Worker, Psychotherapist, and Supervisor, my career has spanned diverse settings and roles.

Services

Supervision

Since 2005, I've supervised counseling in various settings, guiding helpline volunteers, supporting staff aiding homeless youth, mentoring therapists, and assisting social work students.

Relationship Counseling

In relationships, therapy provides guidance amidst challenges. While results aren't certain, it equips you with skills to navigate turmoil, fostering a positive path forward with partners, family, or friends.
